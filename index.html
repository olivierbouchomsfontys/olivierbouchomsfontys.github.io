About Vitality Living Lab

The group of people with an inactive lifestyle is growing. Obesity in combination with other related diseases leads to a huge increase in healthcare costs and loss of productivity. How can you tempt people to be healthy and active? That is what the Vitality Living Lab project has come up with for four years.

With the Sports & Technology Foundation as the driving force, the Cluster Sports & Technology (cluster S&T) has gradually been created in recent years from the strength of the Brainport region, by combining the ambitions and opportunities of sports, government, knowledge institutions and the business community. Partners from the cluster want to capitalize on this favorable starting position by actively seizing (market) opportunities. It wants to strengthen the ecosystem, and thus realize a sporty and vital society with both social and economic impact.

A fundamentally different approach is required to respond to the social challenges and economic opportunities in the field of sport and vitality. The classic form of linear innovation has only very limited effect due to the complexity of the care and health chain.

That is why within the Vitality Living Lab project 14 cluster partners work together to strengthen our regional innovation ecosystem around sport and vitality and to promote a healthy active lifestyle in society. Cluster Sports & Technology is the initiator and project leader of this project.
